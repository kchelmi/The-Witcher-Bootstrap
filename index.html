Location

Kaer Morhen

Kaer Morhen (corruption of Elder Speech: Caer a'Muirehen, meaning Keep of the Elder Sea) is an old keep where witchers of the School of the Wolf used to be trained.

Novigrad

Novigrad is a free city within Redania and is therefore not subject to the rule of that kingdom. It is one of the major ports on the continent and populated by nearly 30,000 inhabitants.

Blaviken

Blaviken is a country town in Arcsea, located on the shore of the Great Sea. During the time of the infamous Butchering it belonged to King Audoen's realm and later was incorporated.

Vergen

Vergen is a dwarven town on the Aedirnian border and is introduced in The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings. Vergen has a unique architectural style, houses and buildings are carved in the rocks.

Why This Movie?

The Witcher is a good fantasy series that opens for a larger story arc for many seasons to come. It is an introduction to characters like Ciri, Yennefer, and Geralt.

Season 1
Geralt of Rivia, a mutated monster-hunter for hire, journeys toward his destiny in a turbulent world where people often prove more wicked than beasts.
Season 2
The eight episodes of The Witcher season 2 are set to continue the story of the eponymous, monster-hunting Geralt of Rivia, played once again by big-screen Superman Henry Cavill. The season 1 finale ensured that the show’s (sometimes confusing) multiple timelines are now aligned, uniting Geralt with orphaned princess Ciri (Freya Allan) – just as their respective destinies decreed.
Season 3
The official episode count for The Witcher season 3 has yet to be revealed, but based on the other two iterations, it is easy to take a guess at how many chapters the third run will have when it releases. Both the first and second sets of episodes have contained eight exhilarating entries ranging from 47 to 67 minutes in length.
